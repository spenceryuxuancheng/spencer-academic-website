Biography

Mr. Spencer Yuxuan Cheng

Research Assistant (Upcoming)

Department of LST, The Hong Kong PolyU

Mr. Spencer Cheng is an upcoming research assistant in the Department of Language Science and Technology at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He obtained his Bachelor's degree in English studies with a concentration in language technology from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. He was trained as a linguist with programming skills. His current research focuses on Chinese syntax, with a particular interest in telicity, verb duplication and focus movement. He is also concerned about how language studies promotes linguistic justice.
